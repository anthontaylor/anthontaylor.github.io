Things to bring up

Learning how open source communites work.

Understanding more the process of languages.

Interest in programming language design.

Reading more text books

Documentation is huge (obviously)

Be involved in the online community on slack and irc.

Lazy Sequence Abstraction

Map, Filter, Reduce

Writing is like programming. Iterate through.
